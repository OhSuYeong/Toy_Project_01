Motivation 1

The reason I became interested in cloud computing dates back to the Hanium contest organized by the Ministry of Science and ICT in 2022. The goal of the contest was to create an application called "Healthy World" that provides a food ingredient analysis service. At that time, my knowledge of app development was limited, so based on the mentor's advice, I started studying both frontend and backend development. I utilized open APIs from the Ministry of Food and Drug Safety to fetch data for analyzing food ingredient tables. The objective was to develop an application that filters out harmful foods and recommends healthier alternatives based on the user's consumption. To achieve this, handling vast amounts of data was essential, leading me to realize the necessity of cloud services. This sparked my interest in cloud computing. After developing an interest, I began studying cloud services and, while searching for educational programs, came across an advertisement for the Kakao Cloud School at the university library. Recognizing the rarity of such opportunities, I decided to apply. Before entering university, I thought I could self-learn C programming sufficiently. However, participating in contests and collaborating on a team for my graduation thesis made me realize the limitations of self-learning, especially in practical scenarios, through the Hanium ICT mentoring program. Therefore, I believe that the entire curriculum of the Kakao Cloud School will be immensely helpful for my goal of becoming an engineer. Among the courses, I am particularly interested in the Kakao employee mentorship and the industry-tailored project execution. This is because I think these aspects will allow me to gain insights into real development challenges and experiences that are not attainable through self-study.

The curriculum of the Kakao Cloud School may pose challenges that make me contemplate giving up. However, my plan will serve as a pillar of support, helping me overcome any moments of doubt. To achieve this, I am determined to successfully gain admission to the Kakao Cloud School.
